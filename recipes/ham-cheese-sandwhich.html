Ham & Cheese Sandwhich

Description

Here I'll show you the tastiest way to whip up a classic Ham and Cheese Sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Place bread on plate
  2. Add a slice of ham on top of one of the slices of bread
  3. Place a slice of cheese on the other
  4. Place slices together with the cheese and ham facing each other
