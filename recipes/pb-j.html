PB & J

Description

Nothing fancy, just a classic. Either smooth or crunch peanut butter is acceptable. Classically, the jelly is either strawberry or grape.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Place bread on plate
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Place jelly on the other
  4. Place slices together with the jelly and peanut butter facing each other
